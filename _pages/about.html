--- permalink: / layout: archive title: "About me" excerpt: "About me" author_profile: true redirect_from: - /about/ - /about.html --- I am a final-year Ph.D student in the Computational Imaging Group with the Department of CSE at Washington University in St. Louis. CIG is a super cool place, where we develop cutting-edge technologies for computational imaging. Before I joined the lab, I received B.Eng. and M.S. from Sichuan Univiesity and Washingtion University in St. Louis in 2015 and 2017, respectively. I love my college, where I had an extremely colorful life. Also, the city Chengdu (where my college locates at) is ‘a city you will never want to leave once you come.’ Currently, my research of interests include imaging with deep learning, PnP/RED algorithms, large-scale optimization, and implicit neural representation. Feel free to follow me if you think my research is interesting! Reading books is my biggest hobby. You can find my reading list here (Chinese version, English verison soon).
